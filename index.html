Introducing Kathait Wedding Point

Located in Jamnikhal, Devprayag, Uttarakhand, we are the ultimate destination where luxury hospitality meets natural Himalayan beauty.

Kathait Wedding Point is strategically situated near the holy town of Devprayag, where the rivers Alaknanda and Bhagirathi merge to form the Ganges. Surrounded by majestic mountains, our venue provides a dreamy, serene backdrop for your most cherished life celebrations.

Whether planning a grand wedding, corporate conference, seeking a peaceful retreat in our luxury homestay rooms, or maintaining your health routine at our state-of-the-art gym, we guarantee exceptional service, elegant atmosphere, and traditional hospitality.
